¿Quién soy?

Hi, everyone! My name is Angela and I'm from Argentina. I love teaching, sewing, doing handicrafts, listening to instrumental music, playing violin and more. But my favourite hobby is cold porcelain modelling. I took up this hobby when I was 10 years. I remember watching an Argentinian tv program called "Utilisima", which used to teach diferent hobbies, and since then that I fell in love with "porcelana fría". I am so thankful to the many teachers that gave me diferent pieces of advice related to this subject and taught me how to improve this beatiful hobby. First, I would like to thank Leticia Suarez del Cerro. She was the one who inspired me to create lovely pieces. Second, I would like to thank Erica Ferrari. She taught me how different materials can merge to make a unique piece born. Last but no least, I would like to thank Monica Parolini who welcomed me in her workshop and made me feel at home.

I leave my social media if you want to see more of my work.
